SERVE YOUR COMMUNITY!

JOIN US! on our service adventure! For over 35 years Bruce's army has been helping old ladies cross the street, taking cats down from trees, and mowing neiborhood lawns- free of charge. If your a strapping young lad with far to much time on your hands join the army and get to work!.

How do I sign up?

Bruces army doesnt exist! But if you wont help old ladies across the street for the sake of it please do join this organization! If you wish to sign up, please find a Bruce you know and ask him to join!

Where are meetings held?

Meetings are held nowhere, but if you wish to meet the other members of the army, you can summon them by chanting the phrase "black stapler!" three times at the top of your lungs in the most public place you can find. Members wont all show up at the same time so be sure to ask anyone walking around if they are a member.
