Community

Plot Summary

Community was a six season long TV show with an ensemble cast of characters in a fictional community college. The story introduces Jeff Winger as a morally challenged lawyer returning to school to earn his degrees again, after a judge found his old ones to be phony. Hilarity ensues and the show follows a very outlandish out of the box type of comedy.

About the Writer

Community was written in part and produced by Dan Harmon, who has gone on now to create the hit animated show Rick and Morty" after community as well as starting a couple slightly less succesful shows in between.
